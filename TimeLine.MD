# Zombie Bay Funk Trap — Creation Timeline

March 2025  
Initial conception and rapid prototyping phase. Core ideas formed around
non-pop trap structures, horror-influenced cadence, and cinematic ambience.
Early rejection of conventional genre boundaries.

April–May 2025  
Intensive exploration and consolidation. Sound identity, naming, and
structural constraints begin to stabilize. Focus shifts from experimentation
to repeatability and intentional design.

June–July 2025  
Peak creative output period. Zombie Bay Funk Trap emerges as a cohesive,
distinct framework. Early versions of what would become the “Haunted Hoard”
production logic are established.

August–September 2025  
Formalization phase. Concepts are refined, named, and documented.
Blueprint-style thinking replaces ad-hoc creation. The genre transitions
from an idea into a defined system.

October 2025–Present  
Expansion into documentation, licensing strategy, and AI-assisted workflows.
Current focus is on authorship protection, research alignment, and controlled
release of early artifacts.
